About Us

Our Story

The Optimum Touch Story Since 2006.

We started in 2006 as Optimum Touch Enterprise and today continue as Optimum Touch Sdn. Bhd., with the same mission: help local families and working adults access lawful financing with clear terms and honest guidance.

As the business grew and client needs evolved, we strengthened affordability checks, legal documentation, and face-to-face client briefings so every borrower understands the terms, charges, and overall commitment before signing.

Today, after more than 20 years serving the local community, we continue to modernize while staying grounded in the same values we started with: transparency, respect, and responsible lending.

Why Choose Us

Why Borrowers Choose Optimum Touch

Fully Licensed under KPKT

We operate as a registered and regulated moneylending business, following legal procedures designed to protect borrowers with proper records and accountable processes.

No Upfront Processing Fees

No advance collection is requested before official signing; fees and charges are disclosed in black and white during consultation.

No withholding of ATM cards

No lender should keep your ATM card, PIN, or account control; repayment must stay under your own authorisation for safer and traceable transactions.

Agreement Signing at Our Registered Office

Terms are explained clearly during consultation, and agreements are signed at our registered office only after every charge, schedule, and condition is fully understood.

Direct Payments to Company Only

All payment channels are handled through official company process only, reducing third-party risk and ensuring clean transaction traceability.

Immediate Disbursement After Signing

Once agreement have been signed funds are immediately transfered to borrowers bank account.

Clear Repayment Schedule in Writing

Repayment dates, tenure, and payable totals are written clearly in the agreement so your monthly commitment remains predictable and transparent.

Early Settlement Options

Borrowers may discuss early settlement and receive clear recalculation explanation according to the agreed policy and payment timeline.

LHDN-Stamped Legal Documents

Documentation is prepared and stamped with proper compliance flow so your agreement set is complete, reviewable, and suitable for formal verification.

Trust Standards
